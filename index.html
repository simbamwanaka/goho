About the Cooperative

Ivhu Agro Coop Cooperative is a community of smallholder farmers producing high-quality, sustainably grown vegetables and fruits. We focus on traceability, fair pricing, and reducing post-harvest loss.

Our Mission

Support local farmers, increase market access, and deliver fresh produce to the community.

Sustainability

Soil-friendly practices, water management, and responsible packaging choices.

Cooperative Model

Collective decision-making, profit sharing, and training for smallholder members.
